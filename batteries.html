Battery Recycling & Environmental Benefits

Why battery recycling matters

Battery recycling is crucial for environmental protection and resource conservation. Improper battery disposal can contaminate soil and water with toxic lead and acid. Responsible recycling protects our future.

Environmental Impact:

  • Lead Recovery: 99% of lead from batteries can be recovered
  • Reduced Mining: Less need for new lead mining
  • Water Protection: Prevents toxic lead from entering waterways
  • Soil Safety: Protects soil from acid contamination
  • Energy Savings: Uses 99% less energy than mining new lead

Recycling Statistics:

Over 99% of lead-acid battery components are recycled globally. By choosing responsible battery disposal, you're part of the solution:

  • ✓ 130 million batteries recycled annually worldwide
  • ✓ One recycled battery provides material for 100 new batteries
  • ✓ Recycling saves millions of tons of CO₂ emissions annually
  • ✓ Lead recycling is the most recycled material globally
