Convert organic waste into valuable compost and resources

Hazardous Waste Management

Safe handling and disposal of hazardous materials (Non-Medical)

Hazardous Waste Types We Handle:

  • Chemical Waste: Solvents, paints, adhesives, cleaning agents
  • Electronic Waste: Batteries, circuit boards, CFL bulbs, monitors
  • Oil & Lubricants: Used motor oil, hydraulic fluids, coolants
  • Pesticides: Old or expired pesticides, herbicides, insecticides
  • Laboratory Waste: Research chemicals, expired reagents

Safety & Compliance:

  • Fully licensed and certified handlers
  • Compliance with pollution control norms
  • Trained personnel with protective equipment
  • Specialized transportation vehicles
  • Complete documentation and tracking

Note: We do NOT handle Bio-Medical Waste. For medical waste disposal, please contact authorized medical waste management services.

Disposal Process:

  1. Initial assessment and waste characterization
  2. Proper packaging and labeling
  3. Secure transportation to authorized facilities
  4. Treatment or neutralization as required
  5. Final disposal with complete documentation
